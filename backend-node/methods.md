Here are all the available methods:

Method	Description
quote(symbol)	Current price, market data
quoteSummary(symbol)	Detailed fundamentals, earnings, etc.
historical(symbol, {period1, period2})	Historical OHLCV data
search(query)	Search by symbol or company name
chart(symbol, {period1, interval})	Chart data
autoc(query)	Autocomplete suggestions
dailyGainers()	Top gaining stocks of the day
dailyLosers()	Top losing stocks of the day
trendingSymbols(country)	Trending symbols
recommendationsBySymbol(symbol)	Related stock recommendations
screener(query)	Screen stocks by criteria
insights(symbol)	AI insights / analyst reports
options(symbol)	Options chain
fundamentalsTimeSeries(symbol)	Historical fundamentals over time

Available intrevals:
1m, 2m, 5m, 15m, 30m, 60m, 90m — intraday
1h — hourly
1d — daily
5d — 5-day
1wk — weekly
1mo — monthly
3mo — quarterly
